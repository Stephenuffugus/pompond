🐸 Pom Pond — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

The short version: Pom Pond is a family chore-and-kindness app run by a parent or guardian. We collect only the small amount of information needed to run the app for your family. We do not show ads, we do not use children's information for advertising, and we never sell or rent anyone's data. A parent is always in control and can review or delete the family's data at any time.

Who this is for

Pom Pond is designed for families and is set up and managed by a parent or legal guardian. A grown-up creates the family, adds children, and chooses the chores and rewards. Children use the app under their parent's supervision and account.

What we collect

  • Information a parent enters: a family name, the grown-up's name, each child's first name or nickname and a chosen icon/color, the chores and rewards you create, and a Parent PIN.
  • Activity within the app: chores marked done, Poms earned, and the critters a child collects and combines (the game progress).
  • If you create a cloud account (optional, to sync across devices): a parent's email address and a sign-in credential, handled by Google Firebase Authentication.

We do not ask children for personal information, and we do not require photos, birthdays, location, or contact details. Please use a first name or nickname for children rather than a full name.

What we do NOT do

  • No advertising of any kind, and no use of children's information for advertising or profiling.
  • No selling, renting, or trading of personal information.
  • No behavioral tracking across other apps or websites.

How information is used

Only to run the app for your family: to show your chores and rewards, track Poms and critters, and (if you use a cloud account) sync your family's data across your devices. We keep simple, anonymous, aggregate usage counts (for example, how many chores were completed across all families on a given day) to understand whether the app is working and improve it. These aggregate counts contain no names and no information that identifies any child or family.

Where data is stored

Family data is stored using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication) on Google Cloud infrastructure. If you only use the app locally without creating a cloud account, your family's data stays in your device's local storage.

Parental rights & choices (COPPA)

As the parent or guardian, you can at any time:

  • Review the information in your family — it's all visible in the app's Parent screen.
  • Delete everything: open Settings → Delete our family & data in the app to permanently erase your family's data, or contact us (below) and we'll delete it for you.
  • Refuse further collection by deleting the family and discontinuing use.

How we obtain parental consent

Before any information is saved, the adult setting up the family must affirm that they are 18 or older and the child's parent or legal guardian, that they have read this policy, and that they consent. If a cloud account is created, consent is additionally tied to the parent's own email-verified sign-in, and we record the consent timestamp. Because we collect only minimal, non-sensitive information (first names/nicknames, chores, and in-app progress) and use it solely to operate the app — with no advertising, profiling, or data sharing — we rely on this parental affirmation rather than a more burdensome verification step. A parent can withdraw consent at any time by deleting the family (Settings → Delete our family & data) or contacting us.

Data retention

We keep your family's data only while you use the app. When you delete your family (or ask us to), the data is removed from our systems. Anonymous aggregate counts, which identify no one, may be kept.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify parents in the app.

Contact

Questions, or want us to delete your family's data? Email privacy@lucidwinds.com.
